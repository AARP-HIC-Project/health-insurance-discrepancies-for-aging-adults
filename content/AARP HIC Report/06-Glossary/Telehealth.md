**Term:**  
`Telehealth`

**Definition:** 
Telehealth refers to the use of electronic information and telecommunications technologies to support long-distance clinical healthcare, patient and professional health education, public health, and health administration. It encompasses a broad range of services, including virtual visits, remote patient monitoring, mobile health (mHealth), and health information technology, all aimed at improving access to care and health outcomes.

**Related Terms:**  
- **Synonyms:** Virtual care, e-health, remote health, digital health
