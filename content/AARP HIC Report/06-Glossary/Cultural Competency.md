**Term:**
`cultural competency`

**Definition:**
Cultural competency refers to the ability of individuals or organizations to understand, appreciate, and interact effectively with people from diverse cultural backgrounds. It encompasses an awareness of one's own cultural worldview, knowledge of different cultural practices and beliefs, and the development of skills to adapt communication and services to meet the needs of culturally diverse populations. It is an ongoing process of learning and self-reflection rather than a fixed state.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Cultural sensitivity, cultural humility, cross-cultural competence, diversity and inclusion, intercultural competence
