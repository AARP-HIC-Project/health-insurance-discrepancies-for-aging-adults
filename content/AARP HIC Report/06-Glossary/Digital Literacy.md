**Term:**
`Digital Literacy`

**Definition:**
Digital literacy refers to an individual's comprehensive ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information effectively and ethically using digital technologies and the internet. It encompasses a broad range of skills, including technical proficiency in operating devices and software, critical thinking to assess the credibility and bias of online content, understanding digital ethics and safety protocols, and the capacity to participate responsibly and productively in digital society.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Digital competence, e-literacy, information literacy, media literacy, computer literacy, internet literacy.
