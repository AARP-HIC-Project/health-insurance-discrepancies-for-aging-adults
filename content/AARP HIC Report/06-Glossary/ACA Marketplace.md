**Term:**
`ACA Marketplace`

**Definition:**
The ACA Marketplace, also known as the Health Insurance Marketplace or Exchange, is a platform established by the Affordable Care Act (ACA) of 2010. It allows individuals, families, and small businesses to compare and enroll in qualified health plans, often with financial assistance in the form of premium tax credits and cost-sharing reductions, depending on income and household size. Its primary purpose is to expand access to affordable health insurance coverage.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Health Insurance Exchange, Exchange, HealthCare.gov (for the federal marketplace)
