**Term:**
`Underwriting`

**Definition:**
Underwriting is the comprehensive process by which an individual or institution evaluates the risk associated with a potential client or transaction, determines the appropriate terms and conditions, and ultimately decides whether to accept or reject that risk. In the context of insurance, it involves assessing an applicant's risk profile to establish eligibility for coverage, calculate the appropriate premium, and define any specific policy limitations or exclusions.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Risk assessment, risk evaluation, risk analysis, credit assessment (in finance), due diligence.
