**Term:**  
`cisgender`

**Definition:** 
A person whose gender identity aligns with the sex assigned to them at birth. This term describes the majority of people and is used in contrast to "transgender," which describes individuals whose gender identity differs from their sex assigned at birth.

**Related Terms:**  
- **Synonyms:** There is no direct synonyms for the specific concept, as it's a precise descriptor. "Non-transgender" is sometimes used but is less preferred.
- **Antonyms:** transgender, gender non-conforming, gender diverse
