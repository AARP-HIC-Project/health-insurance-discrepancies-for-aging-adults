**Term:**
`Health Literacy`

**Definition:**
Health literacy refers to the degree to which individuals have the capacity to obtain, process, and understand basic health information and services needed to make appropriate health decisions. It encompasses a range of skills, including reading, writing, numeracy, communication, and critical thinking, applied within the context of health.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** medical literacy, patient education, health numeracy, insurance literacy, health communication, health information seeking
- **Antonyms:** health illiteracy, health misinformation, health ignorance
