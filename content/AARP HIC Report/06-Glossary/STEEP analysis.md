**Term:**
`STEEP analysis`

**Definition:**
STEEP analysis is a strategic planning and environmental scanning tool used to identify and evaluate the external macro-environmental factors that can significantly impact an organization or project. The acronym STEEP stands for Social, Technological, Economic, Environmental, and Political factors, each representing a broad category of external forces. This framework helps organizations understand the broader context in which they operate, anticipate future trends, assess potential opportunities, and identify threats that are beyond their direct control but influence their strategic decisions and operational environment.
