**Term:** 
`The Trump Administration's Big Beautiful Bill`

**Definition:** 
A sweeping legislative package enacted by the Trump Administration, signed into law on July 4, 2025. The bill, also known as the "One Big Beautiful Bill Act," is a comprehensive law that addresses a wide range of policy areas, including tax cuts, spending reforms, immigration, border security, and healthcare. It makes permanent key provisions of the 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, introduces new tax deductions, and includes significant changes to federal programs like Medicaid and SNAP. The legislation also allocates substantial funding for national security and border enforcement initiatives.

**Related Terms:** 
- **Synonyms:** One Big Beautiful Bill Act, OBBBA, Trump's tax and spending bill
