**Term:**
`Race`

**Definition:**
Race is a social construct used to categorize people into groups based on perceived shared physical characteristics, ancestry, or cultural traits. It is not a biological reality with distinct genetic markers, but rather a concept that has been historically and culturally constructed to establish social hierarchies, power dynamics, and systems of oppression or privilege.
