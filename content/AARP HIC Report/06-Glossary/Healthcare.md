**Term:**
`healthcare`

**Definition:**
Healthcare refers to the organized provision of medical care to individuals or a community, encompassing the prevention, treatment, and management of illness and the preservation of mental and physical well-being through the services offered by medical, nursing, and allied health professions. It includes a wide range of services, from primary care and specialized medical treatments to public health initiatives and [[Long-term Care (LTC)|long-term care]].

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** medical care, health services, health system, medical services
