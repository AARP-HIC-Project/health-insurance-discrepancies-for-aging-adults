**Term:**
`Health Care Sharing Ministries`

**Definition:**
Health Care Sharing Ministries (HCSMs) are organizations whose members, who typically share a common set of religious beliefs, agree to share medical expenses in accordance with their beliefs and the ministry's guidelines. They are not insurance companies and are generally exempt from state insurance regulations and certain requirements of the Affordable Care Act (ACA). Members contribute a monthly "share" amount, which is then used to help cover the eligible medical expenses of other members.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** None (it's a specific legal/organizational type)
