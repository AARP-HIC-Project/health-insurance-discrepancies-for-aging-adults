**Term:**
`LGBTQ+`

**Definition:**
LGBTQ+ is an acronym that stands for Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer or Questioning, and others. It serves as an umbrella term encompassing diverse sexual orientations, gender identities, and gender expressions that differ from heterosexual and cisgender norms. The "plus" sign signifies the inclusion of other identities not explicitly listed, such as Asexual, Pansexual, Intersex, and many more, acknowledging the broad spectrum of human sexuality and gender.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Queer (often used as an inclusive umbrella term, though historically a slur), Sexual minority, Gender minority, SGM (Sexual and Gender Minorities)
