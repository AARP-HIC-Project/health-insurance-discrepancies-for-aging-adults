**Term:**
`Healthcare Access`

**Definition:**
Healthcare access refers to the timely use of personal health services to achieve the best possible health outcomes. It encompasses multiple dimensions, including the availability of services, their affordability, the acceptability of services to the population, and the appropriateness of care provided. Effective healthcare access ensures that individuals can obtain the medical care they need when they need it, without undue financial, geographical, or logistical barriers.

**Related Terms:**
-   **Synonyms:** [[Health Equity]], healthcare availability, medical accessibility, access to care, health service utilization (often discussed in conjunction with, though distinct from, access).
