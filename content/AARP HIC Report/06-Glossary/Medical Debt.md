**Term:**
`medical debt`

**Definition:**
Medical debt refers to outstanding financial obligations incurred as a result of receiving healthcare services, treatments, or medical supplies that are not fully covered by insurance or other payment sources. It represents the portion of medical bills that a patient is responsible for paying out-of-pocket, often leading to significant financial strain and potentially impacting credit.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** healthcare debt, medical bills, patient debt, hospital debt
