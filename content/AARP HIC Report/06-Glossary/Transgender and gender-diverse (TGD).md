**Term:**
`Transgender and gender-diverse (TGD)`

**Definition:**
Transgender and gender-diverse (TGD) is an umbrella term encompassing individuals whose gender identity differs from the sex they were assigned at birth. "Transgender" specifically refers to individuals whose gender identity is different from their assigned sex, while "gender-diverse" is a broader term that includes transgender people as well as those who identify outside of the binary male/female gender system (e.g., non-binary, genderqueer, agender, bigender). This term emphasizes the diversity of gender identities beyond the traditional binary and acknowledges that gender is a complex social construct distinct from biological sex.

**Related Terms:**
-   **Synonyms:** Gender non-conforming, genderqueer, non-binary, gender variant. (Note: These are often sub-categories or related terms rather than direct synonyms, as TGD is an umbrella term.)
