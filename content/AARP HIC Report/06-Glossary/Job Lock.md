**Term:**
`job lock`

**Definition:**
Job lock refers to the phenomenon where an employee feels compelled to remain in their current job, despite a desire to leave or pursue other opportunities, primarily due to the need to maintain access to employer-sponsored health insurance benefits. This situation often arises when alternative health insurance options are perceived as unaffordable, inadequate, or unavailable, particularly for individuals with pre-existing conditions or specific healthcare needs.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** healthcare-induced job immobility, employment-based health insurance dependency
