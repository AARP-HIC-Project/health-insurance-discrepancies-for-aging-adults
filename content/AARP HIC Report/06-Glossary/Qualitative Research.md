**Term:**
`Qualitative Research`

**Definition:**
Qualitative research is a systematic inquiry into a phenomenon, typically conducted in natural settings, that aims to understand the underlying reasons, opinions, and motivations. It involves collecting and analyzing non-numerical data, such as interviews, observations, and textual analysis, to gain deep insights into experiences, perspectives, and meanings. This approach emphasizes understanding the "how" and "why" of phenomena, rather than measuring "how many" or "how much."

**Related Terms:**
-   **Synonyms:** Exploratory research, interpretive research, descriptive research (in some contexts), ethnographic research, case study research.
