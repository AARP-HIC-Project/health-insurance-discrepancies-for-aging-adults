**Term:**  
`Mental Health Services`

**Definition:** 
Mental Health Services encompass a broad range of professional interventions, treatments, and support systems designed to diagnose, treat, and prevent mental illnesses, as well as to promote overall mental well-being. These services typically include psychotherapy, counseling, medication management, psychiatric evaluation, crisis intervention, case management, and rehabilitative programs, delivered by qualified mental health professionals.

**Related Terms:**  
- **Synonyms:** Behavioral health services, psychiatric care, counseling, therapy, psychological services, mental healthcare.
