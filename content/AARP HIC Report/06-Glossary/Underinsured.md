**Term:**
`Underinsured`

**Definition:**
"Underinsured" describes a state where an individual or entity possesses an insurance policy, but the coverage limits, scope of benefits, or policy terms are insufficient to cover the full extent of potential losses, damages, or expenses they might incur. This often results in significant out-of-pocket costs for the policyholder when a covered event occurs, despite having some form of insurance.
