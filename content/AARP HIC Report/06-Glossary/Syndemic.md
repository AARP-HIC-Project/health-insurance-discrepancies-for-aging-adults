**Term:**
`syndemic`

**Definition:**
A syndemic refers to the aggregation of two or more diseases or health conditions in a population, which are exacerbated by social, economic, environmental, and political factors, resulting in increased burden of disease due to their interactions. It emphasizes the synergistic interaction between diseases and the social inequalities that drive and sustain them, leading to a greater overall health impact than the sum of individual conditions.
