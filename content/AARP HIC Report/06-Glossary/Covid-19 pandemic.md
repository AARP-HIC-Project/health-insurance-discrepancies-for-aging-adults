**Term:**
`Covid-19 pandemic`

**Definition:**
The Covid-19 pandemic was a global public health crisis caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2), which emerged in late 2019 and rapidly spread worldwide. Characterized by widespread illness, significant mortality, and profound societal and economic disruption, it led to unprecedented public health measures such as lockdowns, social distancing, and mass vaccination campaigns.
