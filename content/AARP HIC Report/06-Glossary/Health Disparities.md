**Term:**
`Health Disparities`

**Definition:**
Health disparities refer to preventable differences in the burden of disease, injury, violence, or opportunities to achieve optimal health that are experienced by socially disadvantaged populations. These differences are often systemic, avoidable, and unjust, stemming from social, economic, and environmental disadvantages rather than individual choices or biological factors alone.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** health inequities, health inequalities, health gaps
