**Term:**
`long-term care (LTC)`

**Definition:**
Long-term care (LTC) refers to a range of medical and non-medical services provided to individuals who have a chronic illness or disability and are unable to perform daily activities on their own. These services can be provided in various settings, including the individual's home, assisted living facilities, adult day care centers, or nursing homes, and are typically needed for an extended period, often indefinitely.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** custodial care, extended care, chronic care, personal care
