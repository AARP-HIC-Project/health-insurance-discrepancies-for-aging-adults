**Term:**  
`preventive care`

**Definition:** 
Preventive care refers to healthcare services and interventions designed to prevent illness, disease, and other health problems from occurring or worsening, rather than treating them after they have developed. These services typically include screenings, immunizations, counseling, and lifestyle interventions aimed at maintaining health, detecting potential issues early, and mitigating risk factors.

**Related Terms:**  
- **Synonyms:** wellness care, proactive healthcare, health promotion, disease prevention, health maintenance
