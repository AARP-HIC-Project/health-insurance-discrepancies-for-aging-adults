**Term:**
`artificial intelligence`

**Definition:**
Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. It encompasses various technologies and methodologies that enable machines to perform tasks typically requiring human cognitive functions, such as learning, problem-solving, decision-making, perception, and understanding language. AI systems are designed to analyze data, identify patterns, make predictions, and adapt their behavior based on new information, often through algorithms and statistical models.

**Related Terms:**
-   **Synonyms:** Machine learning, deep learning, cognitive computing, intelligent systems
