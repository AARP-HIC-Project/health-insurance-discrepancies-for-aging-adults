**Term:**
`Gender Rating`

**Definition:**
Gender rating is the practice of charging different premiums for insurance policies based on an individual's sex assigned at birth. This practice assumes that there are statistically significant differences in health risks, life expectancy, or other factors between genders that justify varying costs for coverage.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Sex-based rating, gender-based pricing, sex-differentiated pricing
- **Antonyms:** Community rating, unisex rating, age-banded rating
