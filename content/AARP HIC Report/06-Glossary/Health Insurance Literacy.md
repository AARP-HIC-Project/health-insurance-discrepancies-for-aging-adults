**Term:**
`Health Insurance Literacy`

**Definition:**
Health Insurance Literacy refers to an individual's ability to understand, evaluate, and use health insurance information and services to make informed decisions about their healthcare coverage and utilization. It encompasses understanding key insurance terms (e.g., premium, deductible, copayment, coinsurance), comparing different plan options, navigating enrollment processes, and effectively utilizing benefits to manage healthcare costs and access necessary services.

**Related Terms:**
- **Synonyms:** Insurance comprehension, healthcare financial literacy, health plan literacy, health benefits literacy.
- **Antonyms:** Health insurance illiteracy, lack of insurance understanding, insurance confusion.
