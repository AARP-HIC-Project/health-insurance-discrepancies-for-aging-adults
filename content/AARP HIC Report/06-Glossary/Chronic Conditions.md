**Term:**
`chronic conditions`

**Definition:**
Chronic conditions are long-lasting health problems that generally cannot be cured completely but can often be controlled or managed through medication, lifestyle changes, or other therapies. They are typically defined as lasting for a year or more and requiring ongoing medical attention or limiting daily activities.

**Related Terms:**
-   **Synonyms:** Non-communicable diseases (NCDs), long-term conditions, persistent health problems
-   **Antonyms:** Acute conditions, temporary illnesses, short-term ailments
