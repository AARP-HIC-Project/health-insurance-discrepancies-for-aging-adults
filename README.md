# AARP HIC Report
## Description

This research project investigates disparities in health insurance coverage among individuals aged 40 to 65+ and across various demographic characteristics. The study examines the types of insurance available, how these differences affect access to healthcare services, and whether existing coverage meets the specific health needs. 

Given the increased vulnerability to chronic and age-related conditions during this life stage, the project also aims to identify how these discrepancies impact health disparities and health outcomes. In addition, the research evaluates the implications of these disparities for healthcare organizations providing care to aging populations, identifying areas for further investigation.
